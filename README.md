This project demonstrates the concept of **Regenerative Braking**, a system that recovers energy during braking and stores it for future use.

Instead of wasting kinetic energy as heat during braking, the motor operates in **generator mode** and converts mechanical energy into electrical energy, which is stored back into the battery.
